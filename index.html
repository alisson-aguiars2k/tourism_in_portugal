Porto

Porto is the second largest city in Portugal, but it has a great hinterland essence. On the other hand, life is quite pulsating with great local shops, several cultural events, many bars and restaurants, and clubs that go on until dawn. Cultural life is quite attractive. New exhibitions appear daily in museums and galleries, theater festivals, cinema, music. People constantly gather to enjoy a beautiful sunset, bars are always full (in a non-pandemic setting), people are cheerful and receptive.

Coimbra

The cradle of Portugal's most cherished traditions, the region of Coimbra is the perfect destination to immerse yourself in nature's secrets, explore the memory of the most beautiful stories of our country and discover the treasures of Baixo Mondego. Enchanted woods, picturesque villages and refreshing beaches await you. Come and discover the region of Coimbra and along such central paths, let yourself be carried away by its unique charm.
